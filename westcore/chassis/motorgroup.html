MotorGroup(const std::initializer_list<okapi::Motor> &imotors, const std::shared_ptr<okapi::Logger> &ilogger = okapi::Logger::getDefaultLogger())

A group of V5 motors which act as one motor (i.e. they are mechanically linked). A MotorGroup requires at least one motor. If no motors are supplied, a std::invalid_argument exception is thrown.

Parameters

  • imotors – The motors in this group.

  • ilogger – The logger this instance will log initialization warnings to.
